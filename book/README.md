## Human Intentions

This book is intended to relay our personal journeys through the land of ColdFusion. These experiences are human; and the discussion about them should feel human as well. You are more than welcome to use AI as an _aide_ in your writing; but your prose _must_ read as human. Phrases like _"honest take"_, _"jagged edge"_, _"load-bearing"_, _"sharp insight"_, _"precise decision"_, and _"important distinction worth mentioning"_ are the bromides of the machine and read as such. You have a personality &mdash; let that funky-ass personality shine through in your writing!
